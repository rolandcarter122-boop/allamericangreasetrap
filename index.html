Frequently Asked Questions

How often should grease traps be cleaned in restaurants across the U.S.?

Most restaurants should have their grease traps cleaned every 1 to 3 months, depending on volume and size.

What happens if I don’t clean my grease trap regularly?

Neglecting cleanings can cause odors, clogs, and even health department fines.

Do you offer grease trap installation nationwide?

Yes, we install and replace grease traps for commercial, industrial, and educational facilities across the United States.
